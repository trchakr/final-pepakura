SKETCHUP BASICS

SketchUp might be new to many of you, but it’s one of the easiest 3D modeling software tools to start with for papercrafts. It offers an intuitive interface and all the essential features for creating simple yet effective designs.

For those looking to take on more complex projects in the future, you can explore advanced tools like Blender or Mesopotamia, which offer even more flexibility and functionality.
