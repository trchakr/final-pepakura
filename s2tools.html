TOOLS & SOFTWARE

Creating your own template requires three key tools: SketchUp, Pepakura Designer, and GIMP.

Don’t worry—all are free or offer free versions!

Tools Overview

  • SketchUp: Design your 3D model.
  • Pepakura Designer: Unfold your model into a printable pattern.
  • GIMP: Add final touches and decorations to your design.

Need help installing or setting up? Check out their individual pages for detailed guides—or skip ahead if you’re ready to dive in!
