WHAT ARE PEPAKURA PAPER CRAFTS?

Pepakura is a Japanese term that refers to the art of creating intricate 3D models out of paper. By assembling templates, you can construct stunning detailed sculptures ranging from animals to characters and architecture.

Websites like Canon Creative Park have made Pepakura more accessible worldwide by offering free templates and instructions, opening this Japanese craft to a broader audience.
