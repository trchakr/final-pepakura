DESIGN YOUR MODEL

Now that you’ve learned the basics, it’s time to design your very first model! To guide you through this process, I’ve created a simple design—a shape that resembles a face... or a house (it looks a little weird)—that we’ll use throughout this tutorial.

Key Tips for a Flawless Design:

  1. Avoid Dangling Pieces
    • Ensure all shapes are fully enclosed. Any unconnected or open-ended elements can cause issues during assembly.
  2. Holes Need to Be Deleted
    • If your design has holes (e.g., for windows), delete them to make it easier to connect pieces during the building process.
  3. Fix Overlapping Shapes
    • Remove any overlapping sections inside your design. Overlapping parts can interfere with unfolding your model in Pepakura later.

Happy with your design? Let’s move on to the next step and start unfolding it!
